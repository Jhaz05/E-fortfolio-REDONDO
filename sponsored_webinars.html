DATA PRIVACY

Reflection

Conducting the data privacy webinar was an overwhelming experience for us, as we were the ones who organized and prepared the set-up for the event. The topic of data privacy is incredibly important and relevant, given the increasing amount of personal information that is being collected, shared, and used by organizations. The webinar discussed the various laws and regulations that govern data privacy, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the potential consequences of not complying with these laws. It also highlighted the different types of personal information that need to be protected, such as names, addresses, social security numbers, and financial information, and the different ways in which this information can be collected, shared, and used by organizations. Overall, organizing and conducting this sponsored webinar was an overwhelming experience for us, as we felt a sense of responsibility to raise awareness about data privacy and the need to protect personal information.
